about helena qin




Helena is a third year Computer Science major at the University of California, San Diego (also a passionate foodie) whose interest lies in human-computer interaction and engineering for humanity.

She sees computer science as an art form, where the possibilities to create, and change the way we think and live are limitless. Sites habitually found in her browser history include TED and Yelp. She is fond of independent coffee shops, Parks and Recreation, jazz music and often exhibits symptoms of wanderlust.
